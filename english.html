Cabina da cudeschs (in English: book booth) is a project started in 2016 to repurpose an empty telephone booth in Guarda, Engadin, into a public micro-library. Locals and tourists are invited to take a book or leave at any time. In addition, the plan is to host several literary events a year.

The Cabina is one of many public micro-library being created and maintained by volunteers all across Switzerland and worldwide.
